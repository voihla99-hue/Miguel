QA · Coaching · Automation

Automated QA & coaching system

Problem

Performance reviews were ad-hoc and verbal — no scoring rubric, no documented feedback, no way to track whether coaching was landing or patterns were repeating across agents.

Built

A structured QA framework: conversations graded against a locked rubric on fixed scoring bands, a strike ledger tied to a progressive discipline policy, and per-agent Slack feedback cards drafted automatically by an AI-powered first-pass pipeline. Supervisors review and approve before anything reaches the agent. Prior audit results are carried forward so recurring patterns are flagged as repeats, not treated as new.
